Welcome to Black Hole.

Black Hole is a test number war game where players take turns placing counters, 1 then 2 then 3, all the way down to 10. At the end of the game there'll be one place left on the board, that is the Black Hole, which sucks in all the numbers around it. So whoever has the least number pulled in is going to be the winner.
